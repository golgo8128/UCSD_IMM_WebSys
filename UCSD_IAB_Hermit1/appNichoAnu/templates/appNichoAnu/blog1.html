BBQ in a park in San Diego

In San Diego, you find a lot of parks with BBQ facilities. If you see a concrete trash can with the red sign "HOT COALS ONLY", probably there is a barbecue grill nearby.

A grill in a park is usually dirty. You may want to wrap the grill by aluminum foil.

Then start fire.

Now it's time to cook!
