An Ultimate Rough Aggregation of Metabolic Map
Highly Energetic Republic Managing Information Technology (HERMIT)

Metabolism is a chemical conversion of substances in the cell. For example, after eating, carbohydrates in the food will be turned into glucose and it will be uptaken from intestinal tract. Then the glucose will be chemically converted to ATP, a molecule that stores energy for our body, through multiple steps. The metabolisms are very important for the cells to live, and provide a lot of information on how the organisms maintain their lives. In fact, metabolic disorders arise from abnormal metabolism in the human body. Thus, huge amount of researches have been conducted until today by research institutes over the world to elucidate the metabolic pathways inside the cells.

There are a lot of excellent publicly available databases that store information about metabolites or metabolic pathways. However, most of the information out there are unnecessary for us, and a chunk of information which is really necessary for the purpose of our laboratory is often split into multiple databases.

Highly Energetic Republic Managing Information Technology group (HERMIT) is trying to integrate these separated information that are necessary for the activities of our laboratory into our own database, and to add our own information which we got from our research. The stored information can be browsed and modified through our custom interface.
